Official KoG Logo

Story

2006
The team was born 4 years ago with their creators Sangohan, Nero and Mileur. It make a long time that you can see for the first time the tag =KoG= on the net. In fact the team =KoG= was born on the famous game "Grand Theft Auto San Andreas Multiplayer" known as "MTA race". The team had twenty active members from France, Belgium but also from Quebec. At this time the rivalry between MTA servers were very high and the battles were hard.

2007
This year was already the end of the KoG. A major update has affected the world of MTA, this update was not welcome for all. At same time more and more private servers appear on World of Warcraft and the Leader and the founding members go farming mobs. Server =KoG= is stopped and the members find themselves without forum and server. The end. A real deception for all of us because the atmosphere in the team was extraordinary.

2010
June : I install again GTA San Andreas on my computer. And i found a Multiplayer mode very evolved and the possibilities in scripting and mapping are so enormous. I joined a team called Innovation Gaming or IG . Quickly abandoned by the leader. I left it. An idea flashed through my head. Why not set up my own server ? Three days after my session I was owner of an MTA server of 12 players max. I immediately contact the old leader Nero to ask him if he allows me to give up his team and the response was positive. The =KoG= were Come Back.

The following is not a surprise, I begin by contacting old members not without difficulty. And to my delight they are starting to come back and reform the team of Killer Of Gta with the exception of a few players. With the people I met on different servers, the server have more and more maps and qualities scripts. There is a userpanel system with which you can buy little things, a system of points, a game of luck and lots of other little things that put the server to the ranking (ranking server) 43/2000. A very nice place and I must say "thanks" to Blackstar Maikill, Valou, Timic, Diegofkda, designers who do a tremendous job. The team has a new Teamspeak server offered by one of its members, a plus for a team (thank you Valou). At The forum is very active now. I also thank all members =KoG= old and news one because without them KoG simply would not exist ... The Team is growing, and Valou & Maikill have implemented a uploader of maps where players can submit their maps to the hope found one day on =KoG= Server.

March 2011
The team must have more players so a second server 32 opens up. For a total capacity with both servers of 64 players
